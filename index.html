Health Insurance Risk Calculator Instructions

Follow these steps to evaluate the health risk of a customer:

  1. Enter the customer’s height in feet and inches.
  2. Enter the customer’s weight in pounds.
  3. Enter the customer’s age.
  4. Select the customer’s blood pressure category.
  5. Check any family disease history (Diabetes, Cancer, Alzheimer’s).
  6. Click the "Submit" button to calculate the risk score.

The system will calculate the total risk score and display the risk category.
